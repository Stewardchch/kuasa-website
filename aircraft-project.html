Aircraft Design Project

The KUASA Aircraft Design Project focuses on conceptual aircraft engineering and aerodynamic design. Members explore aircraft performance, aerodynamics, structural design, and simulation tools used in modern aerospace engineering.

Focus Areas

  • Aerodynamic analysis
  • Conceptual aircraft design
  • Structural modelling
  • Flight simulation
